Ultrasound with Deep Learning

Reducing Noise in Ultrasound Imaging with GANs.

Currently, ultrasound scanners are susceptible to many sources of image degradation. Post-processing techniques are often required to reduce noise and generate an image that a doctor can evaluate with. Unfortunately, commercial post-processing algorithms are often proprietary. As a result, I worked with researchers at Duke to develop a mimic of this image processing technique with U-Net architectures.

Below is a demo of MimickNet. We developed a model in Tensorflow and ported it to Tensorflow.js for mobile and edge device use. Simply upload a raw ultrasound scan and the model will reduce noise artifacts from the image.
